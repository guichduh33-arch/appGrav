Recipe Instructions

01.

Laminate the détrempe with AOP butter using 5 single folds, resting for 45 minutes between each cycle at 4°C.

02.

Roll to 2.5mm and bake between two perforated mats at 175°C for 22 minutes until deeply golden brown.

03.

Dust with icing sugar and caramelize at 230°C for 2 minutes. Cool completely before assembly.
