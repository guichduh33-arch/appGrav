# Design System: The Breakery Redesign (Luxe Dark)
**Project ID:** 6226570993221239944

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
The design is "Luxe Bakery" – a premium, minimalist, and artisan aesthetic. It uses a dark, moody foundation with rich gold/brass accents, evocative of a high-end French pâtisserie.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Primary Background:** Deep Onyx (#0D0D0F) - `var(--theme-bg-primary)`
* **Surface/Cards:** Warm Charcoal (#1A1A1D) - `var(--theme-bg-secondary)`
* **Accents:** Artisan Gold (#C9A55C) - `var(--color-gold)`
* **Text High Contrast:** Stone Text (#E5E7EB) - `var(--theme-text-primary)`
* **Text Muted:** Muted Smoke (#9CA3AF) - `var(--theme-text-secondary)`
* **Borders:** Subtle Border (#2A2A30) - `var(--theme-border)`

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Display/Headings:** Playfair Display (Serif)
* **Body/UI:** Inter (Sans-serif)

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons:** Shape is `radius-md` (subtly rounded). Primary buttons use a gold gradient and uppercase text.
* **Cards/Tabs:** Use `radius-lg` and `shadow-md`. Active tabs should use gold accents instead of blue.
* **Navigation:** Clean, high-contrast links with gold hover effects.

## 5. Layout Principles
High whitespace, elegant margins, and a sense of "premium density."
